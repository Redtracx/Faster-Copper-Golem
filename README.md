# Faster Copper Golem
<img src="https://github.com/PixelIndieDev/Faster-Copper-Golem/blob/main/documentation/icons/faster-copper-golems-icon.png?raw=true" width="400" height="400">

**Ever felt like copper golems take too long to sort items?**

No more, this mod lets you speed up the copper golem, making them more efficient at moving items between chests.

## Features:
_configurable through [mod menu](https://modrinth.com/mod/modmenu)_
- Customizable interaction time | faster pick-up and drop-off of items.
- Customizable maximum carry size | decide how much a golem can carry at once.
- Customizable search radius | control how far copper golems can scan for chests.
- Customizable movement speed | controls how fast copper golems walk to chests.
- Customizable max amount of chests to check | control how many chests they check before giving up

## Config Options
### Interaction time options
- Vanilla | 60 ticks
- Fast (default) | 30 ticks
- Faster | 15 ticks
- Fastest | 3 ticks

### Maximum carry size options
- Minimum (Vanilla) | 16 items at once
- Default | 24 items at once
- Max | 64 items at once

### Search radius options
- Vanilla (default) | 64x16x64 blocks
- Large | 128x32x128 blocks
- Larger | 160x50x160 blocks
- Largest | 200x100x200 blocks
- Extreme | 1000x200x1000 blocks

### Movement speed options
- Vanilla (default) | 1.0x speed
- Fast | 1.2x speed
- Faster | 1.5x speed
- Fastest | 2.0x speed

### Max amount of chests to check options
- Vanilla | 10 chests
- Many (default) | 25 chests
- More | 50 chests
- Most | 120 chests
- Extreme | 1024 chests
